## 3. Phase 0 deliverable — the design doc

Key content landed in `specs/PHASE-0-DESIGN.md`:

- **Positioning (§1):** gate-shaped receiver binding RFC-0001 §5.1 (boundary) and Ward v0.2 (four gates); extends — does not replace — the `coven` daemon. **Gate 4 fail-closed stated as line-one conformance property** (Nova non-negotiable).
- **Vocabulary (§2, Echo v0.1.1 revision):** metaphor terms bound to concrete referents —
  - **Thread** = authority relationship `surface → writer`, with tension (holds/frays/snaps under load);
  - **Weave** = enforced pattern of threads; Ward's gates are the *loom*, not threads;
  - **Strand** = fiber inside a thread (`ContentHash`, `Signature`, `ManifestEntry`, `AuditTrail`, `SerializationMarker`); fraying = legible intermediate failure;
  - **Channels** = load axis: `Deliberate`, `Forced` (WARD-C1–C6), `Serialization` (new C7), `Mutation`.
- **Predicate-authoritative rule (§2.2, Echo):** `PatternPredicate::coherent` is authoritative; `PatternDescriptor` is derived, for legibility only. Gating enforcement on the descriptor is the named anti-pattern.
- **Four invariants (co-designed, not stacked):** identity-as-memory-property · structural mutation authority · two-compaction contract · survives serialization (lives at strand level via `SerializationMarker`).
- **Explicit anti-goals:** not a policy engine, not a serialization format (Phase 3), not `.af`-compatible (documented divergence 2026-07-14).
